Some random facts about hamsters

🐾 Hamsters can store food in their cheeks—up to half their body weight!

🌙 They’re nocturnal, meaning they’re most active at night.

🌀 Hamsters love running—some can sprint up to 5 miles on a wheel in one night!

🐹 There are 18 species of hamsters, but only a few are kept as pets.
